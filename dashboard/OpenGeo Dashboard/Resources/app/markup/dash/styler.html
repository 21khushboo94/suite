Styler

Styler brings the power of desktop GIS styling to the web, providing an easy-to-use graphical interface to customize the look of your maps.

Styler allows you to craft maps with the look and feel your applications require. By abstracting traditional GIS styling components and adding an easy-to-use web interface, designing maps no longer hinges upon tedious XML coding.

Styler implements the Styled Layer Descriptor (SLD) standard for web map styling to provide rapid prototyping and viewing of styles. Built entirely with JavaScript and leveraging the OpenLayers and GeoExt libraries, Styler's emphasis on open standards makes map styling easy and more efficient.

Further information is available in the Styler Documentation.
